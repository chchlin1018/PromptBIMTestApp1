# === 英文 Prompt ===

1. Build a simple 2-story rectangular house with 3 bedrooms upstairs and a living room, kitchen, and bathroom downstairs

2. Create an L-shaped 3-story office building, each floor about 300 sqm, with open office areas and 2 meeting rooms per floor

3. Design a single-story warehouse, 40m x 20m, with one large open space and a small office in the corner

4. Build a 5-story apartment building with 4 units per floor, each unit has 2 bedrooms, a bathroom, kitchen, and living room

5. Create a 2-story retail building with shops on ground floor and offices on second floor, total area 500 sqm

8. 蓋一間單層鐵皮廠房，長50公尺寬30公尺，裡面分三個工作區

10. 設計一棟4層樓的集合住宅，每層3戶，每戶約30坪，含兩房一廳一衛

12. 蓋一棟醫院大樓，地上8層地下2層，需要特別安全梯、緊急用電梯、完整消防系統和無障礙設施
